Cakes Cakes Cakes
Cake is a flour confection made from flour, sugar, and other ingredients and is usually baked. In their oldest forms,
cakes were modifications of bread, but cakes now cover a wide range of preparations that can be simple or elaborate and
which share features with desserts such as pastries, meringues, custards, and pies.
